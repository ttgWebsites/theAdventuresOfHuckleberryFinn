Characters

  • Huckleberry Finn
  • Protagonist and narrator
  • He is thirteen years old and the son of the town drunk in St. Petersburg, Missouri
  • Forced to learn things on his own and also with out an education
  • He enjoys adventure and spending time with his friend Tom
  • Tom Sawyer
  • Friend of Huck Finn also is the main character in the Adventures of Tom Sawyer
  • Tom completes Huck because he is things that Huck is not
  • Always sticks to what society sees is right and uses them to guide him
  • Widow Douglas and Miss Watson
  • Two sisters that have a lot of money and live in St. Petersburg
  • They adopt Huck
  • Miss Watson, more of the disciplinarian where as Widow Douglas, lets him get away with things
  • Even though she is more lenient, Huck fears disappointing Widow Douglas not Miss Watson
  • Jim
  • One of Miss Watson's slaves
  • Very smart, nice and mature
  • Since he is a different race, people often over look his selflessness and kind heartedness
  • Pap
  • The father of Huck and town drunk
  • He beats Huck and doesn't like that he wants to be educated
  • The duke and the dauphin
  • Con men that are rescued by Huck and Jim when they are being run out of river town
  • The oldest is dauphin “son of King Louis XVI”, the younger one is duke, “Duke of Bridgewater”
  • Even though Huck and Jim see through their lies, they still help them because they too needed help at one point of their lives
  • Judge Thatcher
  • Also takes care of Huck along with Widow Douglas
  • Is in charge of keeping the money that Tom and Huck find safe
  • Huck signs his money over to the Judge when he hears that his father is in town
  • The Grangerfords
  • When Jim and Huck separate, Huck is taken in by these people
  • They invite him to stay in their home and take care of him
  • The Wilks family
  • When the duke and dauphin hear of a man named Peter Wilks that has died, they pretend to be the Wilks brothers in order to scam his fortune from the Wilks sisters
  • Silas and Sally Phelps
  • Tom's aunt and uncle
  • Sally is Tom's aunt's sister Polly
  • They have Jim and try to return him to his rightful owner
  • The only functional family in the novel
  • They end up being too much for Huck because he doesn't like that they abide by society
  • Aunt Polly
  • Tom's aunt and guardian
  • She properly id's Huck who is pretending to be Tom
  • And Tom who is pretending to be his brother Sid
