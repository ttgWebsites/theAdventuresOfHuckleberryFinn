Interesting Details

  • Twain initially intended to write the novel as following the character Huck Finn from the end of the Tom Sawyer story, through adulthood. However, after beginning this, and then a hiatus from writing the novel, he decided to just write the novel the way it is currently.
  • The novel was initially banned in various locations, libraries, and book sellers, as it was seen as being very obscene, particularly referring the protagonist and his behavior.
  • The great author Ernest Hemingway thought so highly of the novel, he went on to claim that it was instrumental to American literature, however he would add that believed it went on for too long, thus hurting the quality overall.
